Disable the Menubar Icon

Please follow the below instructions to disable the icon in the menu bar.


Do you see the Dalton logo in the menu bar? (It looks like this: )
If you are on a Mac Hold control and click it. If you are on Windows, right click it.. Next clck "Hide Button."

To re enable it, please click the three lines menu, then click "More Tools," and Extensions. Scroll down to CoursesPlus, and click "Show Button" (Next to allow in incognito).
