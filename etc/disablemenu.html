Disable the Menu bar Icon

Please follow the below instructions to disable the icon in the menu bar.

  1. Find the Dalton logo in the menu bar. (It looks like this: )
  2. If you are on a Mac, hold control and click it.
  3. If you are on Windows, right-click it..
  4. Click "Hide button".
To re-enable the button:
  1. Click the three lines ("hamburger") menu in the top right.
  2. Select "More Tools" and click on "Extensions".
  3. Scroll down to CoursesPlus, and click "Show Button" (right next to "Allow in incognito").
